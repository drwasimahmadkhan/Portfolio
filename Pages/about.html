Biography

A Journey in
Academia

Dr. Wasim Ahmad Khan is a distinguished Associate Professor of Computer Science. With a career spanning over a decade, his research has focused on the intersection of Cloud Robotics and Cognitive Artificial Intelligence.

After receiving his Ph.D. from NCBA&E in 2020, he joined the faculty at leading institutions. His work is widely cited in top-tier journals, and he has served as a keynote speaker at numerous international conferences.
